Our Mission

As AI agents become ubiquitous, organizations need visibility into what's running on their networks. CogniWatch provides comprehensive AI agent detection, helping security teams discover, monitor, and secure AI deployments before they become vulnerabilities.

The Problem

AI adoption is exploding, but most deployments happen without security team oversight:

  • Shadow AI - developers deploying agents without authorization
  • Misconfigured agents exposing sensitive APIs
  • Default credentials and authentication bypasses
  • No visibility into AI infrastructure
