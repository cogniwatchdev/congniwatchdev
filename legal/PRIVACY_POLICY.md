## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address (if you contact us or sign up for updates)
- **Support Communications:** When you report bugs, request support, or contribute to the project
- **Voluntary Submissions:** Feedback, reviews, testimonials, or feature requests

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

CogniWatch is **self-hosted software**. When you run CogniWatch on your own infrastructure:

#### What We DO NOT Collect:
- ❌ No telemetry data
- ❌ No scan results sent to us
- ❌ No network topology information
- ❌ No AI agent configurations
- ❌ No credentials or authentication data
- ❌ No personal data from scanned networks

#### What May Be Logged Locally (On Your System Only):
- ✅ Scan timestamps and duration
- ✅ Detected services and ports
- ✅ Vulnerability findings
- ✅ Error logs and debugging information

**All data remains on your local system unless you explicitly choose to share it.**

### 1.3 Third-Party Services

If you interact with third-party services through CogniWatch:
- **GitHub:** If you contribute code or report issues
- **Discord:** If you join our community server

These services have their own privacy policies.

## 3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

### 3.1 Where Data is Stored

CogniWatch stores data in the following locations:

- **Local Storage:** All scan results and logs are stored on your local system at:
  - `/home/cogniwatch/data/` (or your configured data directory)
  - This data never leaves your system unless you explicitly share it

- **Email Communications:** If you contact us, your email and our response are stored in our email system

- **GitHub:** If you create issues or pull requests, that data is stored on GitHub's servers

### 3.2 Data Retention

We retain information as follows:

- **Local Scan Data:** Retained until you delete it (you control this)
- **Email Communications:** Retained for 7 years for legal compliance
- **Support Tickets:** Retained for 3 years after resolution
- **Website Analytics:** Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics standard)

### 3.3 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** HTTPS/TLS for all web communications
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Updates:** Prompt security patches and updates
- **Vulnerability Management:** Active monitoring for security issues

**However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.**
