## 1. NOMINATIVE FAIR USE

CogniWatch displays logos and trademarks of AI agent frameworks **solely for identification purposes** under the doctrine of **nominative fair use**. This is standard practice for security scanning and network monitoring tools.

### What This Means:

- ✅ We use framework names and logos to **identify** detected software
- ✅ This use is **factual and descriptive** — not misleading
- ✅ We do **not** imply endorsement or affiliation
- ✅ We do **not** use trademarks as our own brand identifiers
- ✅ This use is **necessary** to convey accurate information to users

### Industry Standard:

This approach is consistent with established security tools:

- **Shodan.io** — Displays logos of detected services (Apache, Nginx, etc.)
- **Nessus** — Shows vendor logos in vulnerability reports  
- **Censys** — Uses trademarks to identify internet-facing assets
- **Nmap** — Displays service/product names and versions
