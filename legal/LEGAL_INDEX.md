## ⚖️ **Legal Overview**

### What CogniWatch Is

CogniWatch is a **network security scanning and monitoring platform** designed to:
- Detect self-hosted AI agents on local networks
- Identify security vulnerabilities in AI agent deployments
- Provide real-time security posture monitoring
- Track agent activity and behavior patterns

### What CogniWatch Is NOT

- ❌ Not a penetration testing tool
- ❌ Not an exploit framework
- ❌ Not designed for unauthorized access
- ❌ Not a surveillance system for end-users
- ❌ Not a replacement for comprehensive security audits

### Intended Use

CogniWatch is designed for:
- ✅ Self-hosted AI agent operators
- ✅ Network administrators monitoring their own infrastructure
- ✅ Security researchers with explicit authorization
- ✅ Organizations auditing their own AI deployments

### Prohibited Use

CogniWatch must NOT be used for:
- ❌ Scanning networks you don't own or operate
- ❌ Unauthorized access to AI agents
- ❌ Harassment, stalking, or surveillance without consent
- ❌ Competitive intelligence gathering
- ❌ Any illegal activity or violation of computer crime laws
